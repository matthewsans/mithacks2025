PART
III — NARRATIVE
State below in reasonable detail why Forms 10-K,
20-F, 11-K, 10-Q, 10-D, N-CEN, N-CSR, or the transition report or portion thereof, could not be filed within the prescribed time period.
Pacific Green Technologies Inc. (the “Registrant”)
was unable, without unreasonable effort or expense, to file its Annual Report on Form 10-K for the period ended March 31, 2025 (the “Annual
Report”) by the June 29, 2025 filing date applicable to smaller reporting companies due to the delay in catching up with filing
of prior periods’ financial statements.
Following filing of the 10-K Annual Report for
the year ended March 31, 2024 on June 20, 2025, the Registrant is in the process of finalizing the 10-Q quarterly financial statements
for the quarters ending June 30, 2024, September 30, 2024 and December 31, 2024.
The Registrant plans to file the Annual Report
once the 10-Q quarterly financial statements for the preceding quarters have been filed, and is in the process of compiling required information
to complete the Annual Report for the year ended March 31, 2025.
The Registrant anticipates that it will file the
Annual Report no later than the fifteenth calendar day following the prescribed filing date.
