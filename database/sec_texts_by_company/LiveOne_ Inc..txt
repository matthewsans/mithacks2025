PART III - NARRATIVE
State below in reasonable detail why Forms 10-K,
20-F, 11-K, 10-Q, 10-D, N-SAR, N-CSR, or the transition report or portion thereof, could not be filed within the prescribed time period.
LiveOne, Inc. (the “Company”) has
not completely finalized its financial statements for the period ended March 31, 2025, and the Company’s certifying auditors require
additional time to complete their audit of the financial statements to be included in the Company’s Annual Report on Form 10-K for
the fiscal year ended March 31, 2025 (the “Form 10-K”). Accordingly, the Company cannot complete and file the Form 10-K by
the due date of June 30, 2025 and needs additional time to finalize its audited financial statements and complete such audit process,
but expects the audit of the financial statements to be completed and the Form 10-K to be filed as soon as possible, on or prior to the
fifteenth (15th) calendar day following the prescribed due date.
